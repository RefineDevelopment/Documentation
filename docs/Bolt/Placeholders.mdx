Here you can find Bolt's PlaceholderAPI placeholder and in built placeholders,
which are used in the scoreboard and tablist.

## PlaceholderAPI

These are PlaceholderAPI placeholders and can be used in messages, menus, etc.
They also let you display information in other plugins.

### üéÆ Queue Placeholders

Can be used to create your own custom queue menu.

Placeholder                                | Returns                                         | Example
------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- | -------
`%bolt_queue_profile_queueing%`            | Total solo players in queue                     | `12`
`%bolt_queue_profile_playing%`             | Total solo players in matches                   | `8`
`%bolt_queue_duo_queueing%`                | Total duo teams in queue                        | `5`
`%bolt_queue_duo_playing%`                 | Total duo teams in matches                      | `3`
`%bolt_queue_<queue_type>_<kit>_queueing%` | Players in specific queue type and kit          | `7`
`%bolt_queue_<queue_type>_<kit>_playing%`  | Players fighting in specific queue type and kit | `2`
