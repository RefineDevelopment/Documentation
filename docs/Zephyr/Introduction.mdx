Zephyr is a fully customizable FFA plugin to suit your servers needs.

:::warning
Zephyr requires a MongoDB database to work
:::

:::warning
Zephyr does NOT depend on Phoenix or CarbonSpigot
:::

## Installation and setup

<Tabs queryString="type">
    <TabItem value="spigot" label="Paper" default>
        1. Download and install Retention, which can be found on the resource page, into your plugins folder.<br/>
        2. Start your server and wait until it finishes booting up, once finished, stop your server.<br/>
        3. Open loader.yml in Retention's plugin folder and enable Zephyr, save and reboot.<br/>
        4. [Setup your arenas](Setup/Arenas/)
        5. [Setup your kits](Setup/Kits/)
        6. You're good to go!
    </TabItem>
</Tabs>
